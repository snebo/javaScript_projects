==Tic Tac toe==

Welcome to Tic-Tac-Toe! Start by entering both players' names to personalize your game. The players take turns clicking on the squares of a 3x3 grid, aiming to align three of their marks (X or O) horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. The game ends when a player achieves this or when all squares are filled, resulting in a tie. Simple, fun, and perfect for testing your strategy skills!
